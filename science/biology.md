# Biology
## Microorganisms
**Microorganisms**/**Microbes**: living organisms that are so small that they can't be seen without a microscope

**Microorganisms** (Major 4):
- **Bacteria**
- **Fungi**
- **Protozoa**
- **Algae**

**Non-Microorganism**:
- Virus
