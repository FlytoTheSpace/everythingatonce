### Temperature

$$T(x)$$

| Unit        | Unit (Expanded)       | Absolute Zero              | Water Freeze              | Water Boil                |
| :---------: | --------------------- | -------------------------- | ------------------------- | ------------------------- |
| `K`         | **Kelvin**            | $0 \text{K}$               | $273.15\text{K}$          | $373.15\text{K}$          |
| `°C`        | **Celsius**           | $-273.15 \degree \text{C}$ | $0 \degree \text{C}$      | $100 \degree \text{C}$    |
| `°F`        | **Fahrenheit**        | $-459.67 \degree \text{F}$ | $32 \degree \text{F}$     | $212 \degree \text{F}$    |
| `°R`, `°Ra` | **Rankine**           | $0 \degree \text{R}$       | $491.67 \degree \text{R}$ | $671.67 \degree \text{R}$ |

**Conversion**:

$$T(\degree C) = T(K)  - 273.15$$
$$T(\degree F) = T(\degree C) \cdot \frac{9}{5} + 32$$
$$T(\degree R) = T(\degree F) + 459.67$$
