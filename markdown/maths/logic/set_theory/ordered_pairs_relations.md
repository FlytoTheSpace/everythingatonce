

# Ordered Pairs

definition:

$$
\newcommand{\dom}{\text{dom}}
\newcommand{\ran}{\text{ran}}
$$

$$(a_0, a_1, a_2, ... a_n) = (b_0, b_1, b_2, ... b_n) \iff a_0 = b_0 \land a_1 = b_1 \land a_2 = b_2 \land ... \land a_n = b_n$$

---


$$(a, b) = \{{a}, \{a, b\}\}$$


**Cartesian Product**:

$$A \times B = \{(a, b)\ |\ a \in A \land b \in B\}$$

**Relations**:

$$R \subset A \times B$$

**Functions** $f$: are Relations such that:

$$\forall a \in A \exists ! b \in B[(a, b) \in f]$$

$$\dom(f) = A$$

$$\ran(f) = \{r | r \in B \land \exists a[a \in A \implies (a, r) \in f]\}$$
