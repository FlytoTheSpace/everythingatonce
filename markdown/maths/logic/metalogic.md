# Meta-Logic

### Therefore

$$a \therefore b$$

### Because

$$a \because b$$

### Asserted

Asserts the truthfulness of the statement $a$

$$ \dashv a$$

### Proveable

Describes the Proveability of $b$ via $a$.

$$a \vdash b$$


### Entails

$$a \models b$$
